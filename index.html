About SILVEREL

Hi, I'm Shirel — diamond specialist and the founder of Silverel. As a jewelry designer, I’ve always believed that each piece should carry meaning, elegance, and personal expression.

On October 7th, I faced a life-changing event. From that moment on, I chose to live fully and with purpose. That decision gave birth to Silverel — a brand built on strength, identity, and resilience.

At Silverel, every creation is designed to tell a story — your story. Because every woman holds a unique journey, and every piece of jewelry should reflect that.
